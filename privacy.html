Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026  |  Effective: May 30, 2026

HitWizard ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered music marketing platform at hitwizardai.com and any associated services (collectively, the "Service"). Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the Service.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use HitWizard, you may provide us with the following categories of information:

  • Artist Information: Artist or band name, genre, mood, similar artists, target audience, song titles, and lyrics you enter into our platform
  • Campaign Data: Budget amounts, marketing goals, platform preferences, and campaign parameters you configure
  • EPK Information: Biography details, hometown, influences, achievements, website URLs, and social media links
  • Account Information: Email address, password, and profile information if you create an account
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers
  • Communications: Any messages you send us via email or support channels

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access our Service, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns, and interaction data
  • Device Information: Browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers, and screen resolution
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, referring URLs, and error logs
  • Cookies and Local Storage: We use browser localStorage to save your form inputs and campaign history locally on your device

1.3 Information from Third Parties

When you connect third-party accounts to HitWizard (such as Meta, TikTok, Google, or Spotify), we may receive:

  • Account identifiers and access tokens necessary to perform actions on your behalf
  • Public profile information from the connected platform
  • Analytics and performance data from your connected ad accounts

We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide the Service features you activate.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To generate AI-powered marketing campaigns, EPKs, content, hooks, and other outputs based on your inputs
  • AI Processing: Your song information, lyrics, and artist details are transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API to generate marketing content. This transmission is necessary to provide the core functionality of HitWizard
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account, process payments, and manage subscriptions
  • Platform Publishing: Where you have connected third-party accounts, to publish content or campaigns on your behalf
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve our features
  • Communications: To send you service updates, security alerts, and promotional communications (with your consent where required)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security incidents

3. THIRD-PARTY AI PROCESSING

Important: HitWizard uses Anthropic's Claude AI to generate marketing content. When you submit information through our platform, that information is sent to Anthropic's API servers for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy governs their handling of this data. We encourage you to review Anthropic's Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy. We do not send sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary for content generation.

4. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating the Service, including Anthropic (AI processing), Netlify (hosting), Supabase (database), and Stripe (payments)
  • Connected Platforms: When you authorize HitWizard to publish content on your behalf, we share necessary data with Meta, TikTok, Google, or other platforms you connect
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate confidentiality protections
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of HitWizard, our users, or the public
  • With Your Consent: In any other circumstances where you have given explicit consent

5. DATA RETENTION

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account plus 90 days after deletion
  • Campaign history: Retained for 24 months or until you delete it
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations
  • Local storage data: Stored on your device and controlled entirely by you — you can clear it at any time through your browser settings

6. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal obligations
  • Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hitwizard.ai@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. COOKIES AND TRACKING

HitWizard uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Browser localStorage: Used to save your campaign history, form inputs, and character vault data locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly submit it through the Service
  • Session data: Temporary data used to maintain your logged-in state
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how users interact with the Service

You can control localStorage through your browser settings. Note that clearing localStorage will delete your local campaign history and saved data.

8. SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • API keys stored as environment variables, never exposed in client-side code
  • OAuth 2.0 protocol for secure third-party platform connections
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
